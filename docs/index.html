About Triquetra Trust

A modern digital trust structure combining legal enforceability with self-sovereign identity, tokenized assets, and secure family governance.

Digital Status & Asset Trust

Triquetra Trust is designed to recognize and protect living status through digital means, manage real-world and digital assets, and provide secure governance for family members.

  • Status Correction: Recognize living persons
  • Digital Identity: Self-sovereign credentials
  • Asset Management: Real and digital assets
  • Jurisdiction: Multi-state compatible
  • Token Security: Permissioned blockchain
  • Legal Enforceability: Full faith and credit
  • Family Banking: Internal credit facility
  • Governance: Trustee controlled

The trust utilizes modern cryptographic identity, electronic signatures, and distributed ledger technology while maintaining full legal backing through traditional trust law. All digital records are supported by certified legal documents and can be used as evidence in courts of law.
